About Me

I am a researcher at The Sainsbury Laboratory (TSL) in Norwich, currently pursuing my PhD. As a member of the Proteomics Team under Dr. Frank Menke, I specialize in the molecular mechanisms of plant-infecting fungi.

My primary research interest lies in studying the intricate signaling pathways within Magnaporthe oryzae. By using this model system, I aim to uncover how fungal pathogens sense their environment and coordinate the cellular processes required to colonize host plants.
