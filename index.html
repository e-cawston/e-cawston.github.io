About Me

I am currently researching for my PhD at The Sainsbury Laboratory, Norwich. I am working in the Proteomics Team under Dr Frank Menke on signalling in plant pathogenic fungi using Magnaporthe oryzae as a model system. My work focuses on characterising how MAPK signalling is regulated using multi-omic approaches, as well as genetic, molecular, and cell biology.
